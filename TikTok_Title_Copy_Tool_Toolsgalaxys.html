How to Use This Tool

  1. Open the TikTok app and find the video you want to get the title from
  2. Tap "Share" on the video, then tap "Copy link"
  3. Come back to this tool and paste the link in the input box above
  4. Click "Extract Title" to get the video title
  5. Use the "Copy Title" button to copy it to your clipboard

This tool helps you get TikTok video titles when the app prevents direct copying on mobile devices.
